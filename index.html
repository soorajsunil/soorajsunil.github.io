Academic Background

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Windsor. My research focuses on data-driven identification, learning, and control for energy storage systems, particularly Lithium-ion batteries.

Previously, I completed a Master’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in Mechatronics. My thesis focused on collaborative SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) for autonomous systems.

I hold a Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
